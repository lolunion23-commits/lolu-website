About the Competition关于大赛

Where Neuroscience
Meets the Next Generation神经科学
与新生代的相遇

The BrainAI California Championship is a statewide brain science competition inspired by BrainCo's global NeuroMaster series. Students experience cutting-edge brain-computer interface (BCI) technology — measuring focus, relaxation, and mental states in real time.BrainAI加州锦标赛是一项受BrainCo全球NeuroMaster系列赛事启发的全州脑科学竞赛。学生们体验前沿脑机接口(BCI)技术——实时测量专注力、放松度和心理状态。

Organized by Light of Life Union (LOLU), this event bridges the gap between cutting-edge technology and compassionate education — giving California's youth a unique window into the future of human-computer interaction.由生命微光公益联盟(LOLU)主办，本赛事架起前沿科技与爱心教育之间的桥梁——为加州青少年打开人机交互未来的独特窗口。

  • Real-Time Brain Monitoring实时脑波监测

    Students wear a BCI headset that measures brainwaves and focus in real time.学生佩戴脑机接口头戴设备，实时监测脑电波和专注力。

  • AI-Powered InsightsAI驱动洞察

    Advanced AI algorithms translate brainwave data into meaningful performance metrics.先进AI算法将脑电波数据转化为有意义的表现指标。

  • Inclusive & Educational包容且富有教育意义

    Designed to inspire curiosity regardless of technical background.无论技术背景如何，旨在激发好奇心和探索精神。

Competition Structure大赛结构

How the Championship Works大赛如何运作

The BrainAI Championship is structured to give every student a fair, exciting, and educational experience — regardless of prior science knowledge.BrainAI锦标赛的设计旨在为每位学生提供公平、精彩且富有教育意义的体验，无论其先前的科学知识基础如何。

01

Orientation & Training简介与培训

Students receive an introduction to brain-computer interface technology and learn how to use the BCI headset effectively.学生接受脑机接口技术介绍，学习如何有效使用BCI头戴设备。

Pre-competition赛前准备
02

Focus Challenge专注力挑战

Participants complete guided tasks that measure their concentration and mental focus, scored by AI analysis of their brainwaves.参赛者完成引导任务，AI通过脑电波分析评分，测量专注力和精神集中度。

Main Competition主要赛事
03

Awards Ceremony颁奖典礼

Top performers receive trophies, certificates, and recognition. All participants receive a certificate of participation.优胜者获得奖杯、证书和荣誉。所有参与者均获得参赛证书。

Post-competition赛后环节
Eligibility参赛资格

Who Can Participate?谁可以参赛？

K–12 StudentsK-12年级学生

Open to all students in kindergarten through 12th grade enrolled in California schools or homeschooled in California.对加州公立、私立学校及家庭教育的幼儿园至12年级学生开放。

California Residents加州居民

The championship is open to students residing in the state of California. Future editions may expand nationally.本锦标赛面向加州居民学生开放。未来版本可能扩展至全国范围。

No Prior Experience Needed无需先前经验

No background in neuroscience or technology required. The competition is designed to be educational and inspiring for all.无需神经科学或技术背景。大赛旨在为所有人提供教育和启发。

Individual & Team Entries个人与团队参赛

Students may participate individually or as part of a school team. Detailed rules will be announced with registration.学生可以个人或学校团队形式参赛。详细规则将随报名开放一并公布。
