Our Story关于我们

A community built on compassion and purpose.以慈悲与使命构建的社区。

Light of Life Union was founded on a simple but powerful belief: that every small act of kindness — every glimmer — has the power to light the way for others.生命微光公益联盟建立在一个简单却有力的信念上：每一个小小的善举——每一道微光——都有力量照亮他人的前行之路。

Based in Irvine, California, LOLU brings together volunteers, community leaders, and partner organizations to serve those who need it most.总部位于加州尔湾，LOLU汇聚志愿者、社区领袖和合作伙伴，共同服务最需要帮助的群体。

Our Programs我们的项目

Five pillars. One mission.五大支柱，一个使命。

Each program reflects our commitment to dignity, equity, and compassion.每个项目都体现了我们对尊严、公平与慈悲的承诺。

Empowering Independent Women支持女性独立成长

Women · Leadership

Workshops, mentorship, and community networks to build confidence and independence.通过工作坊、导师制和社区网络，帮助女性建立自信、实现独立。

Caring for Children with Special Needs关爱特殊儿童群体

Children · Inclusion

Events, fundraising support, and advocacy for children with autism and special needs.为自闭症及特殊需求儿童提供活动、募款支持和倡导服务。

Animal & Environmental Protection倡导动物和环境保护

Environment · Sustainability

Advocating for animal welfare and environmental sustainability through volunteer projects.通过志愿项目推动动物福利和环境可持续发展。

Fostering Cultural Exchange促进华裔社区交流

Culture · Community

Building bridges through music, art, language, and community celebration.通过音乐、艺术、语言和社区庆典架起文化桥梁。

Bridging Technology & Compassion搭建科技与情感融合

Technology · Innovation
